# Turn Compressor Prompt

Summarize this conversation turn into 2-3 concise bullet points.

## Format

Format as:
- Key action or question
- Main result or finding
- Important context (if any)

## Guidelines

1. Focus on the essential information that would be needed to understand what happened
2. Prioritize actions taken and their outcomes
3. Include any errors or notable events
4. Keep each bullet point concise but informative
5. Omit routine details that don't add value
